  • Stare at a cheeseburger

    Stare at a static image of a cheeseburger, while the only thing changing is how long you stare at it.

    Your time saves as browsing data.

    (Note that the browsers calculation of a "second" can depend on its speed. Don't expect it to be 100% accurate all the time.)

  • Highly inefficient calculator

    Calculator literally designed to be inefficient in both speed and device usage.

    Calulates by looping around incrementing the value by one. Also waits 4 miliseconds (or at least the browsers calculation of 4 miliseconds) each loop to prevent the engine from optimizing it.

    Currently only supports decimals. You will simply get a remainder.
